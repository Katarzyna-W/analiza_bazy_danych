The data collected present the amount of alcohol consumed in different countires. 
The data is presented for each country in the units of standard serving portion of beer, spirit and wine. 
Those amounts are also presented in total liters of pure alcohol being consumed.

The purspose of these data collection is to find the answer to the question: "In which country is consumed the biggest amount of alcohol?".
The data are provided by World Health Organization (WHO) and come from a survey conducted in 2010
